Practical IT Training, Aligned with Industry Needs

Aratechnology offers practical, industry-aligned IT training with hands-on labs and certification. Choose between short courses and intensive bootcamps — delivered online or on-site.

We provide a range of programs from fullstack web development, advanced programming languages like C++ and Java, to corporate productivity training such as Microsoft 365. Our focus is on delivering skills that can be immediately applied in the workplace.

Training programs are designed for individuals, companies, and educational institutions. We also run specialized programs for beginners, such as basic coding using Scratch, and business optimization through Google My Business.
